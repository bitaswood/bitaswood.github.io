My name is Yiheng Jiang. I am pursuing a PhD degree in the <a href="https://ustc-ivclab.github.io/">Intelligent Visual Data Coding Laboratory</a> at the <a href="https://www.ustc.edu.cn/">University of Science and Technology of China (USTC)</a>. My supervisors are Professor <a href="https://faculty.ustc.edu.cn/lil1/en/index.htm">Li Li</a> and Professor <a href="https://faculty.ustc.edu.cn/dongeliu/">Dong Liu</a>.

My research interests include:
- Data compression
- Multi-modality processing
- Machine learning

My recent research focuses on:
- Multi-modal data compression
- End-to-end optimization techniques for learned data compression
- Practical learned image compression system
